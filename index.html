Goalie

Ways to Improve as a Goalie
  • Work on your catching and holding consistency
  • Work on your punts and goal kicks
  • Work on your communication and know when to come out of the box for the ball
  • Work on your step explosiveness to make farther saves
  • Video

Defense

Ways to Improve as a Defender
  • Work on your 1v1 defending
  • Work on your distribution and decision making
  • Work on your positioning and chemistry with your back line
  • Video

Midfield

Ways to improve as a Midfielder
  • Work on opening up and having a good first touch
  • Check your shoulder
  • Work on having good field vision and capitalizing if you see an opportunity
  • Video

Offense

Ways to improve as a Attacker
  • Work on different skill moves so you aren't predictable
  • Work on holding up the ball when you don't have any options
  • Work on finishing
  • Video
